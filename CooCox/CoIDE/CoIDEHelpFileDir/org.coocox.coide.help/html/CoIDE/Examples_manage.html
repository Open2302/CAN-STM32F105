Managing example

To manage examples using CoIDE, you just need to perform the following steps:

 
  1. 1. Login with your CooCox account.
  2. 2. Find your example in the example view.
  3. 3. Click modify link of the example you want to modify.
  4. 4. you can modify the name, description, code of the example.
  5. 5. Click Save to save your modification.
  6. 6. View the modified content.
  7. 7. You can click delete to delete the example.
  8. 8. Click "Yes" to delete the example.
  9. 9. The example was deleted.
