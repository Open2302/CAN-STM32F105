Modify your Component

To modify a component, you only need to perform the following steps:

 
  1. 1. Login with your CooCox account.
  2. 2. Right Click the component you uploaded in the Repository View, and select Modify.
  3. 3. Modify the content of "Files" page.
  4. 4. Modify the content of "Tags" page.
  5. 5. Modify the help content and click "save" in the "Saving" page.
  6. 6. Congratulation shows when your change is successfully saved.
