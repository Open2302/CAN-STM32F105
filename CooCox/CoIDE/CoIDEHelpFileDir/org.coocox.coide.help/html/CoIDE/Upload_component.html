Upload a Component

To upload a component, you only need to perform the following steps:

 
  1. 1. You can click "Upload Component" under the "Project" menu or the button in the Repository view.
  2. 2. Login with your account, it could be the account of yours in CooCox Forum.
  3. 3. Type the name and descriptions of the new Component.
  4. 4. Add files or folder of the Component.
  5. 5. Click next to save the settings of "Files" and then enter the "Tags" page.
  6. 6. Edit the information about the component; you need to select the manufacture, chip, category for the component.
  7. 7. Edit the Component Dependency.
  8. 8. Edit the help content for this Component.
  9. 9. Click "Commit" to upload the Component to the internet.
  10. 10. The Congratulation will come out if your upload is successful.
